Pokemon Crystal Archipelago in your browser.

A full multiworld client for Pokemon Crystal. You provide a YAML and your own vanilla Crystal ROM; everything else happens locally.

  • Generation and ROM patching run in your tab via Pyodide.
  • Your ROM never leaves this browser. It's stored locally so you don't need to re-provide it.
  • Emulation by binjgb. Saves persist per seed.
  • The multiworld is hosted on archipelago.gg via a tiny proxy.
